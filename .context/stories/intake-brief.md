## Distilled notes
- Product: single-user, self-hosted reading and note-taking app for PDFs and EPUBs.
- Core loop: read → highlight → note → retrieve.
- MVP/v1 scope: all pre-AI work in the PRD (foundation, PDF reader, search, EPUB, imports, polish).
- Post-MVP scope: AI retrieval and later multi-agent/reranking work.
- Implementation starts from scratch.
- v1 target devices: desktop and laptop only.
- v1 exclusions decided during intake: mobile support, cover thumbnails.
- UX priority: smooth PDF reading and very fast highlight-to-note capture.
