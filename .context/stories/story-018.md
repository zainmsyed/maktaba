**Status:** not-started

**Last accessed:** 2026-04-24

## Goal
Add the main user-facing polish needed for daily reading: themes, keyboard shortcuts, and saved reading progress for PDFs and EPUBs.

## Verification
Change the theme, use reader shortcuts, close a document, and reopen it to confirm the last reading location is restored.

## Scope — files this story may touch
- Light, dark, and warm themes
- Reader keyboard shortcuts from the PRD
- Persisted reading progress for PDF pages and EPUB CFIs
- Last-opened metadata updates in the library
- Optional highlight color selection if it fits the session

## Out of scope — do not touch
- Mobile-specific layouts
- Cover thumbnails
- AI chat polish

- Story 013
- Story 014
